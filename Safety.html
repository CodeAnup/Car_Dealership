Safety Features:

1.] Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS)

Advanced Driver Assistance Systems (ADAS) include features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and automatic emergency braking to enhance driver safety.

2.] Crash testing rating

At Horizon Auto Group, our vehicles excel in crash test ratings, providing you and your passengers with the highest safety standards through rigorous impact testing and evaluation, ensuring peace of mind on the road.

3.] Air Bags

Horizon Auto Group vehicles are equipped with advanced airbag systems, including front, side, and curtain airbags, ensuring maximum protection and safety for all passengers in the event of a collision.

4.] Child Safety

At Horizon Auto Group, we prioritize child safety with features like ISOFIX mounts, rear door child locks, and advanced seatbelt systems to ensure the utmost protection for your little ones.

5.] Auto Poilet

Autopilot systems automate driving tasks like steering, acceleration, and braking in certain conditions, enhancing convenience and safety by reducing driver workload, though they require driver supervision for safe operation.
